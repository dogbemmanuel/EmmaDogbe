About Emmanuel, AKA: DOGBEtech:

Greetings! I'm Emmanuel Dogbe, better known in the tech sphere as DOGBEtech . Born and raised amidst the bustling streets of Accra, Ghana, on April 12, 1998. My journey into the captivating world of technology has been nothing short of exhilarating. My passion for programming ignited during my formative years in Dunkwa on Offin, a quaint town nestled in the heart of the central region of Ghana.

It was there that I first encountered the magic of coding, sparking a curiosity that would shape my future endeavors. Following the completion of my basic education in 2015, I embarked on the next phase of my academic journey at Boa Amponsem Secondary School. It was during my time there that I solidified my commitment to pursuing a career in software development, culminating in the attainment of my secondary school certificate (WASSCE) in 2016.

Fuelled by a relentless drive to deepen my understanding of the digital landscape, I sought out opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. This quest led me to OpenLabs Ghana (formerly NIIT ), where I immersed myself in the intricacies of software development. Over the course of my tenure, I dedicated countless hours to mastering various programming languages and frameworks, ultimately earning a Diploma in Software Development in 2022.

The pivotal moment in my professional journey came with an enriching internship at CodeWeb Company Limited in Accra, Ghana, where I had the privilege of working under the mentorship of CEO Mr. Barshar Larry. Initially assuming the role of a Marketing Assistant, I quickly transitioned into a full-time Frontend Developer position, harnessing my creative instincts to craft immersive user experiences. Driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I continually seek out opportunities to broaden my horizons and stay abreast of the latest technological advancements.

Whether it's mastering new frontend frameworks, exploring the intricacies of full-stack development, or delving into emerging technologies, I approach each challenge with enthusiasm and determination. When I'm not engrossed in the world of coding, you'll often find me immersed in research, scouring the digital landscape for innovative solutions and cutting-edge technologies.

I firmly believe that learning is a lifelong journey, and I eagerly embrace every opportunity to expand my skill set and push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of software development. If you're as passionate about technology as I am and are interested in collaborating on exciting projects or discussing groundbreaking ideas,

I'd love to connect with you! Feel free to reach out to me at dogbemanuel@gmail.com, and let's embark on this journey of innovation together.
